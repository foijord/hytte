# Kalsneset 27 — replacement cabin: execution plan

*Prepared 2026-07-17. Property: gnr/bnr 437/109, Kristiansand (Søgne). As-is bank
valuation ~8.5 M. Scope: replace the ~60 m² main cabin + outdoor wing + deck with a
~80 m² catalog cabin (ferdighytte) with hems on a concrete slab, one combined
~19 m² concrete storage/tech room under the new 8×3 m deck. The annex and the
naust stay. Kit assembled by local tradesmen (owner as byggherre).
Planning budget **~3.1–3.2 M kr** (range 2.8–4.5 M). 3D model:
https://foijord.github.io/hytte/ (`?new=1` shows the new build).*

**The one rule that outranks everything: the old cabin stays standing until the
strandsone dispensation and building permit are granted.** The legal basis is
erstatningshytte — replacement of an existing building. Demolish early and the
grandfathered right dies with the building.

## Standing risks

1. **Early demolition kills the erstatningshytte right** — Gate A is absolute.
2. **Ridge height** (+0.97 m) is the likeliest dispensation objection → 25° fallback.
3. **Blasting volume** is unknown until the pad is stripped → contingency exists for this.
4. **Kit scope gaps** — kitchens, hems stair, interior doors often excluded; reconcile quotes line-by-line.
5. **Access logistics** for kit delivery on the coastal road — verify with supplier early.
6. **Asbestos beyond the known eternitt** — miljøkartlegging closes this before it can stop work.
